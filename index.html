About
Khaqi

CONTACT - SEND ME AN EMAIL

I am a dancehall reggae artiste who performs five different genres of music, these are Reggae, Dancehall, Afro, Pop, and Reggaeton.

I began music in 2010 in a music band ("Pumwani High School Band) during my high school years and went solo in 2017 with my first single titled, "Big Up (Bad and Sexy Riddim)".

Over time, I have collaborated with notable artists such as Frankie Dee ("Jam"); Nviiri the Story Teller ("Overdose Reggae Cover"); and Shamir (Love Avenue). I have been interviewed on numerous radio stations such as Vybez Radio, Milele FM, Hot96, Citizen Radio, Homeboyz, and Inooro and also made several TV appearances on programmes such as One Love, Jam Down, The Trend, 10 over 10, and Wirekererie (Inooro TV).
